The studio

A finishing studio for considered interiors.

We treat a wall as a piece to be composed — proportion, material, and light worked out before a single panel goes up. Every project begins with the room as it is, and ends with a surface that looks as though it was always meant to be there.

From grasscloth and hand-printed papers to raised-panel wainscoting and custom cornice work, AP Premium pairs old-world installation craft with a clean, modern eye. One studio, accountable from first measurement to final reveal.

What we do

Three disciplines, one finished room.

01

Wallpaper

Specialty and designer papers — grasscloth, silk, mural, and hand-printed — sourced, matched, and hung with seam-level precision.

02

Molding

Crown, cornice, chair rail, and casing milled and fitted to the architecture, restoring or inventing character room by room.

03

Paneling

Wainscoting, board-and-batten, and full feature walls built as joinery — flush, true, and finished to furniture standard.
